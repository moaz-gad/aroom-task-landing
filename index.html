Welcome Message

In 2015 at COP21, a global consensus was reached to cap the increase in temperature to 1.5°C above pre-industrial levels by 2050. According to scientific findings, this requires cutting emissions in half by 2030, a target we have only seven more years to achieve.

Earth's future hinges on today's climate action.
Climate Reassessment

COP28 UAE presents a critical chance to reassess and refocus our approach to climate change. In collaboration with the UNFCCC Executive Secretary, the UN Climate Change High-Level Champion, and the UAE Youth Climate Champion, my commitment is to foster agreement among nations to propel climate initiatives.

Inclusive Solutions

Our joint efforts will concentrate on speeding up emission reductions through practical energy transition, revamping land use, and altering food systems. We aim to bring forward solutions for nations at risk, implement strategies for loss and damage, and ensure the most inclusive Conference to date.

Climate Change Urgency

As the world grapples with the escalating impacts of climate change, the urgency for immediate and substantial action becomes increasingly evident. Our planet is experiencing unprecedented shifts in weather patterns, leading to extreme events like droughts, floods, and hurricanes with greater frequency and intensity.
